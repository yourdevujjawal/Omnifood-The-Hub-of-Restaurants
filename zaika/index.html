About Us

Zaika Family Restaurant, a wonderful Multi Cuisine Restaurant situated in the close proximity of Laheriasarai, Darbhanga offers the best in hospitality, ambiance, and of course food. The restaurant has club house ambiance with both covered and open area.
Its banquet hall is perfect for partying. Mouth watering dishes at the restaurant are of high quality and they stimulate your taste buds with their fragrance and aroma.
We serve a delicious range of Indian, Chinese, Mughlai and Continental delights. We are keen to provide the best service to our guest at the most economic price.
Don't forget to taste our speciality — The Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani.
