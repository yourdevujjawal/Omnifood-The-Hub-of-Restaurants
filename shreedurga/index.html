Our features

With the aim of pampering our guests with the best food, Shree Durga Restaurant features a plethora of services.
The restaurant is classified as one of the best veg & non-veg cuisine destination.
We hope that you have a great time with us.

Veg & Non-Veg

Whether you are a vegeterian or you love non-veg, we have a series of items in our menu to fulfill the desire of your taste buds. We are bound to be a paradise for food lovers. You will find a series of Veg foods, Non-Veg foods as well as a range of Sweets and Snacks.

Prime Location

Shree Durga Restaurant is situated at the heart of Laheriasarai, Darbhanga. Its located at Laheriasarai Tower Chowk. One can easily spot the restaurant and can also reach here by asking any shopkeeper. We are famous, indeed!

Fully Air Conditioned

Whether it is the scorching heat of june or the coldest December, here, you will always be comfortable. The restaurant is fully air-conditioned and once you enter the restaurant, you would not want to go out anytime. We make sure that you have the best time here.
